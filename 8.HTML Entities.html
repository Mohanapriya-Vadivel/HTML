Today, we will learn about HTML Entities and Special Characters.

Common HTML Entities

  • <   – Less than (<)
  • >   – Greater than (>)
  • &   – Ampersand (&)
  • "   – Double Quote (")
  • '   – Single Quote (')
  • ©   – Copyright (©)
  • ®   – Registered (®)

Example in Text

5 < 10 and 10 > 5

Use & to represent & symbol
